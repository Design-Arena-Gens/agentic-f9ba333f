Employee Bonus Problem - LeetCode Style Challenge

Problem Statement

Objective: Write a MySQL query that reports the name and bonus of every employee who satisfies at least one of the following conditions:

  • The employee has a bonus value that is strictly less than 1000
  • The employee did not receive any bonus (no matching record in the Bonus table)

Input Tables

Optimized MySQL Solution

SELECT
    e.name,
    b.bonus
FROM
    Employee e
LEFT JOIN
    Bonus b ON e.empId = b.empId
WHERE
    b.bonus < 1000 OR b.bonus IS NULL;

Output

Solution Explanation

1. LEFT JOIN: We use a LEFT JOIN between Employee and Bonus tables to ensure all employees are included, even those without bonus records.

2. WHERE Clause: The condition b.bonus < 1000 OR b.bonus IS NULL filters for:

  • b.bonus < 1000: Employees with bonuses strictly less than 1000
  • b.bonus IS NULL: Employees with no bonus record (LEFT JOIN produces NULL for unmatched rows)

  • No unnecessary sorting or aggregation operations
